Research

  • Post-Training and Inference-Time Adaptation of Vision/Language Agents for Exploratory Decision Making

  • Scalable and Multimodal Learning for Out-of-Distribution Forecasting
    • Adaptive Information Routing for Multimodal Time Series Forecasting
  • Science of Learning (e.g. distribution shifts, OOD generalization, data&model biases)

    • Gradient Surgery for One-shot Unlearning on Generative Model
